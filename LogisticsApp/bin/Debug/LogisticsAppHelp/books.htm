Books Page

How can I use the searchbar?

In order to find a book through the searchbar you have to know the exact title.

I want to see the summary of a book. Is that possible?

You can click on the title of the book and the link will transfer you to a field with all the details of the book.

I want to buy a book in pdf format but there is no such option

If the book is not available in pdf format, you can contact us through the contact us form and we will reach back to you with an immediate solution.

I have selected my desired books. How can I proceed to payment

In order to reach the payment form all you need to do is to click on the basket icon.

button "search": Press to search for the book you have entered

button "child books": Press to view child books

button "literature books": Press to view literature books

button "crime books": Press to view crime books

button "all": Press to view all books

button "add to basket": Press to add a specific book to the basket

button "basket": Press to show the items you have selected to buy

You can select the title of each book to read its summary
