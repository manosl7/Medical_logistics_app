Coffee shop Page

I have selected a product and I want to remove it from my order sheet.

You can select the product you wish to remove and simply click the button "remove"

How can I receive my order

After you press the button "order entry and payment" you will receive a message with a code. Then you can head to the cashier of the coffee shop for the payment and receipt of your order.

button "add": Press to add an item to the order

button "remove": Press to remove an item form the order

button "clear": Press to clear all the items from the order

button "order entry and payment": Press to enter your order and pay
