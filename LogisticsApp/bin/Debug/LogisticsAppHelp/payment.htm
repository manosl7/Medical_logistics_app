Payment Page

What are the available payment methods?

There are two options. You can either pay in cash or you can choose to pay by card. In case you choose to pay in cash you should fill in your name and your surname. On the other hand if you choose to pay by card you apart from your name and surname you should also fill in the details of your card.

I have chosen a product that I want to remove from the basket. Is it possible?

Yes, you can click on the tname of the product you want to remove and then press the button "remove".

How can I receive my order?

There are two ways of receiving your order.
1.You can receive it from the store
2.You can receive it from home with an extra charge of 2.5 euros by completing your address information.
